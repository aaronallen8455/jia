What is Jazz in Austin?

Jazz in Austin is a place where Austin jazz musicians can post where and when they'll be performing, and where jazz fans can find out about all the events happening here in Austin, Texas.


Austin is fortunate to be home to many outstanding jazz musicians. The goal of Jazz in Austin is to help promote these artists by providing a hub for all things jazz in the 'Live Music Capitol of the World.'

How does it work?

View Shows and Concerts by day from the Calendar

Learn About Individual Artists on the Musician Profiles page

Discover Jazz Hot Spots on the Venues page

How do I sign up?

Musicians and promoters who have registered an account can log in and create events. Musicians can also make a personal profile page, add a bio, and manage their upcoming shows.
