Welcome to Jazz in Austin!

What is Jazz in Austin?
This is a place where Austin jazz musicians can post where and when they'll be performing, so that you'll always know what live jazz is happening here in Austin, Texas!

How does it work?
- Use the calendar on this page to view all of the events for any given day. Click on an event's title to view the details for that event.
- Go to 'Musician Profiles' to find out about specific artists in the Austin jazz scene and to see where they'll be playing next.
- 'Venues' has information about places you can go to hear live jazz, including a list of upcoming performances.

How do I add my events to the calendar?
Musicians or promoters wanting to add events must first register an account. Once you've done that, you can log in and create your event. If you're a musician, you can also use your account to make a personal profile page, where your bio and upcoming shows will be displayed.
